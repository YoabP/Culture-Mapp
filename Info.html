Verde Park
Verde, meaning green, Park is a beautiful park, named after its bastful vegetation. Originally it was named "Jesus Salvador" (Saviour Jesus) when it was founded in 1987, but due to its vivid color, people called it "the green park". Its name was officially changed to "Parque Verde" in 2003. The park is a great place to spend time, looking at the gorgeus landscape, or participating in out-door activities.
